Five months have passed and half of the company trip to Cyprus have almost passed... I am writing this on a boat trip and enjoying being with my colleagues and the view is amazing. When this is published I might be on my way back to Sweden. But let's start from the beginning...

After 11 hours of travel I finally arrived to the hotel and meet all my colleagues that I have never meet and it was awesome to finally meet people that iI have never meet and what can I say that more than that they are amazing in so many ways.

First evening at the hotel was amazing we had a great dinner with a head waiter that was amazing.

The day after was the first full day and I woke up and realised that I was going to present today so after a few minutes of adjustments I headed down for a breakfast that is like a two part breakfast. First part is cheeses and hams together with bread. The second half is a small menu with a lot of different egg dishes. We started with a welcome talk with info about the week we where about to have on Cyprus and in Limassol. Then it was my time to talk about productivity and how I try to accomplish that. After me we had lunch and we did go through the roadmap. After that it was time to head out on the streets of Limassol with a guide and the city is really nice specially the old parts.

Second day already and the hardest day for me because we was outside like all day and I am not used the brights sun or the heat. But making food together instructed by a local was really nice even if it was to hot sometimes for me. This evening we did eat at a what felt like a popular place even for the local people and we ordered metze which basically is a lot of food.

Now it is Sunday and usually this is my lazy day and it started lazy for me because I did not go to beach, instead I stayed at the hotel with two of my colleagues and did some small work things... We had a quick lunch and then we headed out for a boat cruise which was amazing, it gave me a huge feeling of freedom. My biggest fear was it was going to be hot but cause of the wind of the boat moving it was not instead it was just perfect for a guy like me coming for the Scandinavian countries.

Monday and a new week starting, we started it with talking about what values we have inside of Novu and the we talked about personas. After lunch we had a R&D workshop and it was great and I think we need to do more of those kind of stuff...
That evening we had a calm snack and beer session together in the lobby of the hotel and it was really nice to have something more relaxed.

When I am writing most of this post Tuesday was yesterday and it was a very interesting day with communication, marketing and product workshops.

In the evening we headed out to get asian food and specific Sushi in the end I ended up having baous and buns and they tasted good. After the asian we did go to the bar for a few drinks, a few people and me stayed a bit longer and had ice cream from Israel before we did go to the hotel and I felt a sleep.

In my last post I wrote "people that are amazing remote must be even more amazing IRL right" and I let me tell you I was not even close to be right the people I have had the pleasure to spend a week with and it is hard to go home and seeing these people more then online becase I feel as if we really connected during this week.
With that said I hope I soon can see most of them in Israel in a couple of months. I have learned a lot of being in a company where all of the people care about everyone and about the company. My view on Novu members is that they are in Novu not because it is just a work it is something much bigger and something that every single one of us is putting everything into.

I know there will be another meet up as this next year and that I will go to Israel soon but this will leave a feeling of emptiness and do not get me wrong here I am not sad or anything it just a feeling that tells me I had an amazing time and now I am going back to normal that feels a bit boring but I know we need it to enjoy weeks like the week I just had.

A door that I like to call Novu opened almost 10 months ago, this week have opened alot of doors between the members of Novu and the door for the future of Novu is wide open.
