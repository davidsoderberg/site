Okay so now it is like beginning of January and Gali my collegue in Israel invites to her wedding in Israel in beginning of February and I check flight tickets and so but at this point I really liked to go but it feelt like to much.
After like a week I changed my mind and talked to another collegue and he invited me to stay at his place around the wedding.
We decied to keep it secret to Gali and told her boyfriend, oh boy what hard this was for me to keep it secret to Gali for like two weeks.

Time passed and the situation in Israel got worse then it was when I booekd the tickets, but with the great support of my parents and some collegue I decied to go anyway (this post is a proff that i survived).

So Saturday I traveled to Copenhagen to stay at the hotel to be on airport on time early Sunday to take a flight to Tel Aviv again.
My collegue meet me at the airport and we traveled to his and his very nice wife apartment, I made myself at home and in the evening we had kebab and visited a local rock concert with another collegue.

Monday and the day of reveal the sureprise to Gali, I was a bit nervous to be honest we. We spent the day on an open space office where you can pay to work from for a day.
After that we did go to this amazing bar (Bellboy) everyone arrvied before Gali and I had my eyes facing the entrence door so I could see Galis face when she did see me.
And that face made the whole trip alone, every time I think of it, it makes smile. She was so happy and surprise that to see me being in Israel.
We had some great drinks at the bar and moved on for pizza and beer and that pizza was very very big and so good in the night.

Tuesday was a calm working day from the apartment and we made Swedish meat balls in the evening.

Next day we did go to the office again and some other collegues showed up as well, after working day was over me, Dima and Tomer did go to an amazing restaurant with food and service. One of the best restuarant in israel I have heard :P

Thursday and the day of the wedding we worked from the apartment until time of the wedding. We got to a very nice venue and they served a lot of great small food snacks and we got some good wine.
The cermony was in hebrew so you guessed it I did understand every word... not :P But it was very touching and very beuitifull.

Friday and we visited the food market in Tel Aviv and had a lot of good food and some wine with a collegue and his wife.
After it we did go home and I did not do much until I fell a sleep.

It is already Saturday and we did go north to visit a collegue of us and his amazing wife. It was a very great day with walks, cookies, board games and sushi.

It is now today and the day started with a coffe with Dima and Time, then I headed of to the airport again.

Thanks to everyone and special thanks to Gali for the kind invitation to her wedding (Wish you all the best in your marriage) and Dima that hosted me this time and gave me a lot of great food.

To me Novu is my second family, sometimes you are tired of them and most times you wish you could be with them all the time. That is the thing that makes Novu special, an collegue and friend of mine said it great about Gali, "Gali is ... Gali (as an adjective)".
And I think that is true about every of the people working in Novu we are very unique and to describe any of us our name is the best way I think.

Every time I have switched job I have stayed 3 times longer in the next one, that would mean I will stay 15 years in Novu and I hope that will be the case.
Yes we have stuff to improve and stuff to figure out but looking back at my other to places it is about the feeling of being part of something much bigger and I do in Novu.
All issues and problems we can solve togther and the feeling of us being family (at least for me) is a great start to be able to solve any of those heading our way.

Go Novu!
